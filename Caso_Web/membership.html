Membership

CASO membership is open to every Adventist student in tertiary education within the East Kenya Union Conference — come as you are, grow together.

Qualifications

Who can join CASO?

Membership is open and inclusive. The only requirement is being a student in a tertiary institution within the EKUC catchment area.

🎓

Active Member

Any student enrolled in a tertiary institution, private college, or university within the East Kenya Union Conference who is a baptised Seventh-day Adventist.

Full rights & voting
🤝

Associate Member

Any student in a CASO-covered institution who is not yet baptised but is interested in the Adventist faith and wishes to participate in CASO activities and mission.

Participate & serve
⭐

Honorary Member

Conferred by the CASO central executive on individuals — including non-students — who have rendered outstanding service to the organization and its mission.

By executive appointment

Non-Discrimination Statement

CASO does not discriminate on the basis of gender, ethnicity, nationality, economic background, or physical ability. Every student who meets the membership criteria is equally welcome in the fellowship, ministry, and governance of the organization. Our unity is founded on one faith, one baptism, and one Lord — Ephesians 4:5.

Membership fees

Subscription fees support CASO's rallies, evangelism campaigns, and administrative costs. Fees are set by the CASO central executive and reviewed annually.

Current fee amounts are confirmed at each annual main rally and communicated through zone executives and institution chaplains. Contact the secretariat for the latest figures.
