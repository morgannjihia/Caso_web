Evangelism seasons

Two major seasons every year

CASO runs two consolidated evangelism seasons annually — a first-term campaign in March–April and a third-term campaign in November–December — alongside termly Mission Sabbaths in every member institution. These seasons are anchored by the "Go One Million" watchword.
